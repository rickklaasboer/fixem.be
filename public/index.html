fixem.be

Fix broken social media embeds in Discord.

Prepend fixem.be/ to a link before posting it:

https://fixem.be/https://www.reddit.com/r/pics/comments/abc123/example/
https://fixem.be/https://bsky.app/profile/user.bsky.social/post/xyz789
https://fixem.be/https://x.com/user/status/1234567890123456789
https://fixem.be/https://clips.twitch.tv/FunnyClipSlug
https://fixem.be/https://www.tiktok.com/@user/video/7311234567890123456
https://fixem.be/https://www.threads.com/@user/post/AbCdEfGhIjK
https://fixem.be/https://www.instagram.com/p/AbCdEfGhIjK/

Discord's crawler gets a rich embed; everyone who clicks lands on the original post.

Debugging? Append ?fixem=preview to view the embed HTML in your browser.

Not affiliated with any of the linked platforms.
